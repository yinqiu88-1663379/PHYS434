p_low = stats.norm.cdf(-1)
p_high = stats.norm.cdf(1)
lower_threshold = poisson.ppf(p_low, 100) + 300
high_threshold = poisson.ppf(p_high, 100) + 300
print(f"This gives a 1σ uncertainty of {lower_threshold} to {high_threshold} on the true signal strength")

This gives a 1σ uncertainty of 390.0 to 410.0 on the true signal strength
